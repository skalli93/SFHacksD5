Tree Planting

San Francisco, CA

  1. A CHANGING LANDSCAPE - it's no secret that our world has changed in the past few years. More people, more cars, more emissions. Help us plant trees to offset some of these emissions and beautify our city.
  2. EASY AND SIMPLE - just dig and plant. No experience needed. All ages welcome but children must be accompanied by adults. For this project to have an impact, we need a commitment of 25 individuals willing to donate their time.
  3. NO TIME, NO PROBLEM - too busy to come out and help us? No problem. Simply make a tax deductible contribution below to help fund the trees and equipment that will be used on that day. After we're done, equipment will be donated to nearby schools and community centers to encourage them to plant more trees.

There are acres of damaged forest due to the California wildfires. We need the people of Wine County to come together are rebuild our community.
