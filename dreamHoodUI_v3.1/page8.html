Grocery Store

Richmond, CA

  1. FOOD DESSERT - Parts of Richmond don't have a grocery store with fresh food for miles. This leaves residents to rely on fast food restaurants which can often be detremental to their health and well being.
  2. AFFORDABLE - The grocery store will make prices affordable for the local community, any profits will be used to expand the offerings and subsidising of healthy food. 
  3. SELF SUSTAINING - This projects provides a well needed community project that is backed by a business model with the health of the people in mind, not profits. 

Our community needs fresh healthy options. Let's come together and make it happen.
