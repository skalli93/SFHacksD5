Beach Cleanup

Montrey Bay, CA

  1. NO RESPECT - Trash covers the beaches we all enjoy and no one wants to do something about it. Until now.
  2. TIME FOR CHANGE - let's take action. Let's do what others have been too lazy to do. Time to make our beaches shine and bring them back to their natural beauty. We need volunteers to clean the beaches and have fun with us. 
  3. NO EXCUSES - we all enjoy our beaches on those gorgeous summer days so it's time to give back and get involved. We need your help to clean up these beaches. We're hoping to get about 50 volunteers to make the biggest impact and restore our beaches to a pristine condition.

I walk on the beach every day and notice so much trash that could be cleaned up if we put our heads together. Let's do something about it to keep our dogs and children safe and make it happen.
